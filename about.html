About

Hey there! I'm Yash, a data scientist who loves building AI systems that actually solve real problems. For the past few years, I've been deep in the world of natural language processing and large language models, figuring out how to make machines understand and work with human language in meaningful ways.

I get excited about the challenge of taking messy, unstructured data and transforming it into something useful. Whether it's building systems that can extract insights from millions of documents, creating AI agents that understand what you're asking for, or fine-tuning models to be really good at specific tasks, I'm always looking for smarter ways to bridge the gap between data and decisions.

When I'm not training models or optimizing pipelines, I'm probably exploring the latest developments in generative AI, tinkering with new frameworks, or learning about how to make AI systems more efficient and accessible, or you will find me scrolling X following and finding amazing threads on ML.
